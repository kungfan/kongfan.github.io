About Me

I am kongfan,a pharmacist in HMHC. I have been since 1987 in hubei province. I am also a programer , a linuxer, and a bunch of other things too . I spend most of my time on learning python ,reading books and doing some small project with python .I like creating software because i've always dream of being a engineer to make something to change other people's life. I love trying some new technologies ,those make me exciting !

In 2010 , I earned my bachelor's degree with Honors in pharmacy from HBUST . and then worked as a chemical engineer at xianju pharmaceutical Co.,Ltd which is the bigest steroids and hormones manufacturing in China. In 2013, I got an opportunity to work for HMHC,so I relocated to HuangZhou for staring my new life .

I write something here every day . I hope doing this as long as I can. this blog is my diary , my memo , my bar where i was the only bartender.
